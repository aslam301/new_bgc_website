## 3. Refund Policy

```markdown
# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** [DATE]

This Refund Policy explains our refund procedures for BoardGameCulture services.

## 1. General Policy

**No refunds are provided except in cases of technical failure or platform error.**

By using our platform, you acknowledge and accept this policy.

## 2. Event Tickets

### 2.1 No Refunds
- Event ticket purchases are **final and non-refundable**
- This applies to both free and paid events
- Refunds are **not provided** for:
  - Change of mind
  - Inability to attend
  - Event cancellation by organizer (unless otherwise stated by organizer)
  - Weather or transportation issues

### 2.2 Technical Failures (Platform Error)
Refunds **may** be issued if:
- Payment was processed but registration was not completed due to platform error
- Duplicate charges due to technical glitch
- Event page showed incorrect information at time of purchase

**To request a refund for technical failure:**
- Contact support@boardgameculture.com within 48 hours
- Provide transaction ID and description of the issue
- Include screenshots if applicable

### 2.3 Event Organizer Discretion
- Individual event organizers may have their own refund policies
- Such policies will be clearly stated on the event page
- BoardGameCulture facilitates but is not responsible for organizer-issued refunds

## 3. Marketplace Transactions

### 3.1 No Refunds
- Marketplace purchases are **final and non-refundable**
- Sales are between buyer and seller
- Refunds are **not provided** for:
  - Change of mind
  - Found a better price elsewhere
  - Buyer's remorse

### 3.2 Item Not Received or Not as Described
If you do not receive the item or it is significantly different from the listing:
1. Contact the seller first to resolve
2. If unresolved, open a dispute within 7 days of expected delivery
3. Platform admins will review and mediate
4. Refunds issued if:
   - Item was never sent
   - Item is counterfeit or significantly misrepresented
   - Seller failed to fulfill obligations

### 3.3 Dispute Process
- Submit dispute through your transaction page
- Provide evidence (photos, messages)
- Allow 48 hours for seller response
- Admin decision is final
- Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days

### 3.4 Intermediary Transactions
- If using community intermediary, item must be verified before pickup
- No refunds after pickup confirmation
- Disputes must be raised before pickup

## 4. Platform Fees

### 4.1 Non-Refundable
- Platform fees (3-5%) are **non-refundable**
- This applies even if underlying transaction is refunded
- Payment gateway fees (2%) are also non-refundable

### 4.2 Exception: Platform Error
- If platform error caused incorrect fee calculation, difference will be refunded

## 5. Premium Features (Future)

When premium features are introduced:
- Monthly subscriptions: No refunds for partial months
- Annual subscriptions: Pro-rated refund if cancelled within 30 days
- Technical issues: Full refund if service is unavailable for 7+ consecutive days

## 6. Refund Process

### 6.1 Timeline
- Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Bank transfers may take additional 3-5 business days to reflect

### 6.2 Method
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- If original method is unavailable, bank transfer will be used

### 6.3 Deductions
- Payment gateway charges (2%) are not refundable and will be deducted
- Platform fees are not refundable (except in case of platform error)

## 7. Fraud and Abuse

- Fraudulent refund requests will result in account suspension
- Repeated disputes without valid reason may lead to marketplace ban
- We reserve the right to investigate suspicious activity

## 8. Technical Failure Definition

Technical failure includes:
- Platform downtime during transaction
- Database errors causing data loss
- Payment gateway errors causing duplicate charges
- Server errors preventing service delivery

Technical failure **does not** include:
- User error (wrong event selected, incorrect details entered)
- Third-party issues (internet connection, device problems)
- Force majeure events

## 9. Contact for Refund Requests

To request a refund:
- Email: refunds@boardgameculture.com
- Include: Transaction ID, date, reason, evidence
- Response within 48 hours
- Decision communicated within 5 business days

## 10. Modification of Policy

We may update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes apply to transactions after the update date.

## 11. Governing Law

This Refund Policy is governed by Indian consumer protection laws.

---

**Summary:** Refunds are only provided in cases of technical failure or platform error. Event tickets and marketplace purchases are otherwise final and non-refundable. Disputes for marketplace items not as described will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
```

```markdown
# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** [DATE]

BoardGameCulture uses cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve our services.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function:
- **Authentication:** Keep you logged in
- **Security:** Prevent fraud and protect your account

### 2.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies (Optional - Future)
If implemented, these would be used for:
- **Retargeting:** Show relevant ads
- **Campaign Tracking:** Measure marketing effectiveness

This Cookie Policy may be updated. The "Last Updated" date will reflect changes.
```

## Database Schema for Legal Acceptance

```sql
-- Track user acceptance of legal documents
CREATE TABLE legal_acceptances (
  id UUID PRIMARY KEY DEFAULT gen_random_uuid(),
  user_id UUID REFERENCES profiles(id) ON DELETE CASCADE,

  document_type TEXT CHECK (document_type IN ('terms', 'privacy', 'refund', 'cookies')) NOT NULL,
  document_version TEXT NOT NULL,
  accepted_at TIMESTAMPTZ DEFAULT NOW(),
  ip_address INET,
  user_agent TEXT
);

CREATE INDEX idx_legal_acceptances_user ON legal_acceptances(user_id);
CREATE INDEX idx_legal_acceptances_type ON legal_acceptances(document_type);
```
